## 5. The AI is not a person

EldritchDM's narration is generated by a language model. It can be wrong,
biased, repetitive, or out of context. It is not legal, medical, financial,
psychological, or safety advice — and any in-character "advice" the bot
gives is part of a fictional game, not a real recommendation.

The project enforces a **mechanical-honesty contract**: every die roll, HP
change, AC check, and turn boundary is computed by deterministic Python,
not the LLM. This is a property of the software's design, but the LLM can
still emit incorrect *narrative* claims around correctly-computed mechanics.
Players and Operators should treat narration as flavor, not as binding
fact.
